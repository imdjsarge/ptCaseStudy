The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxary camping experience also included electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located int he lodge.

What should I bring?

Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also packt comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
